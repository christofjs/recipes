1. Butter one side of two pieces of bread and place the butter side of one
   piece face down in a hot pan.
2. Place all of the cheese on top of that piece of bread.
3. Place the other piece of bread on top of the cheese butter side up.
4. Grill until the bottom piece of bread is toasted.
5. Flip the sandwich over in the pan.
6. Grill until the other side is toasted and the cheese is melted.
7. Take it out of the pan.
8. Eat it.
